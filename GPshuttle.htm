Trolley Shuttle Service

Both the St. Petersburg Downtown Looper Trolley AND the Central Avenue Shuttle will operate throughout race weekend at normal rates. (see map)

Regular St Pete Trolley

The Looper provides a circular route between the city's parking facilities, hotels, restaurants and attractions.

Service begins at 7am each day (Friday/Saturday/Sunday) and goes until 12am on Friday/Saturday and 7pm on Sunday. The closest Looper stop to the race course will be at the Hilton hotel located on 2nd Street N. and 4th Avenue S. 

The fare is only $0.50 each time you board.  Children 5 yrs. and under ride free. Exact change please. 

FREE Park and Ride Shuttle from the Trop

There is FREE shuttle service from Tropicana Field parking lot to the race course. Parking at Tropicana Field will be $10.

The shuttle will pick passengers up outside the main parking gate at 10th St. and Third Ave. S. and drop passengers near the Hilton on Fourth Ave. S. between Second and Third Streets. There will be a two-block walk to the race entry gate.

The shuttle will operate all three race days from 7 a.m. to 7 p.m.
