See, Click, Fix

On your smart phone, download this free mobile app. When you encounter a problem, such as an overgrown lot or a tree down in the right-of-way, you can take a picture of the problem with your camera phone and submit it through See, Click, Fix. The city will be notified of the issue for a quick resolution to the problem.
