Prohibited Items

The following is a list of prohibited items. These are not allowed through the race site entrance gates.

  • Audio/Video recording devices
  • Coolers
  • Folding chairs
  • Food and beverage
  • Frisbees, helium balloons, or inflatable items of any kind
  • Golf carts for personal use
  • Glass bottles / containers, cans (including aerosol)
  • Handbills or samples are not to be distributed or placed on the grounds without the expressed written pre-approval of the event organizers
  • Laptop computers
  • Large bags, including but not limited to: duffel and grocery bags, luggage
  • Laser lights and pointers
  • Motorcycles or off-road vehicles (including ATV's, ATC's, dune bug bicycles)
  • Pets (service animals excepted)
  • Skateboards, roller blades, roller-skates, scooters, or bicycles

Source of information: www.gpstpete.com
